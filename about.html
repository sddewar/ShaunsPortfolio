About Me

Shaun has recently completed a double Degree in Information Technology (Majoring in Software Development) and Multimedia (Majoring in Sound Production) at Griffith University. He is also an Assistant Manager at Hungry Jacks managing a team of people at the Upper Mount Gravatt store at Garden City.

He is interested in Interface and Web Design and received a High Distinction in User Interface Design and Distinctions in Web Programming, Virtual Studio Technologies and Dynamic Multimedia Systems. For Shaun’s Industry Project in his final year of study, Shaun held the positions of UI/UX Designer and Developer for the App that his team developed.

Shaun has good communication and people skills and excellent leadership qualities. These traits as well as Shaun's attention to detail were recognized by Hungry Jacks when he was offered to commence his management training after only 18 months working as a crew member. After 6 months of management training Shaun was promoted to Assistant Manager. He regularly trains junior members of staff and handles any customer complaints with professionalism to resolution. His attention to detail and organization skills ensures the store runs efficiently during peak times.

Shaun is eager to commence his IT career in a dynamic company.
